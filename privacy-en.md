# Privacy Policy — YESNO

_Last updated: April 28, 2026_

## TL;DR

**YESNO does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.** All processing happens on your device. Your photos never leave your phone.

## What data we access

YESNO requests access to your **photo and video library** for the sole purpose of:
- Displaying your photos and videos so you can review them.
- Deleting items you explicitly choose to delete (always with your final confirmation).

We do **not** access:
- Your contacts, calendar, microphone, camera, or location.
- Any data outside the photo/video library.

## What data we collect

**None.** YESNO has no servers, no analytics SDK, no crash reporting, and no advertising network. The app does not make outbound network requests for any user data.

## What data we store

The app stores a small amount of **non-personal usage data** on your device only, using AsyncStorage:
- Total photos deleted (count)
- Total space freed (megabytes)
- Number of cleaning sessions completed
- Best session record
- Day-streak counter
- Last session date
- Selected language preference
- Haptic feedback preference

This data **never leaves your device**. Uninstalling the app deletes it permanently.

## Permissions

| Permission | Why |
|---|---|
| `READ_MEDIA_IMAGES` (Android 13+) | Show your photos so you can review them |
| `READ_MEDIA_VIDEO` (Android 13+) | Show your videos so you can review them |

You can revoke these permissions at any time in your device settings. The app cannot function without media access; if revoked, you'll see a screen asking you to grant access or open system settings.

## Children's privacy

YESNO is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Third-party services

**None.** YESNO has no third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising integrations.

## Open Source

The Privacy Policy itself is open source — you can view its history in this repository. The app source is private but you can verify these claims by inspecting network traffic on your device (you'll see no outgoing connections).

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be available at this same URL. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the change date.
